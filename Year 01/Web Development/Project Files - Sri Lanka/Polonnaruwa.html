Polonnaruwa

Polonnaruwa is a one of beautiful and religious place in Sri Lanka. Polonnaruwa situated at Polonnaruwa district in North-central province and this is

the the second largest city in this province.Polonnaruwa is the second oldest kindom in Sri Lanka named as Kindom of Polonnaruwa.

Polonnaruwa rich agricultural area like Anuradhapura. Kings such as King Parakramabahu , King Nissankamalla ,King Vijayabahu have done great

service to build temples, tanks to make polonnaruwa flourish.In the past, Polonnaruwa is lush city like as today. The paddy fields in the town have

contributed immensely to supply rice to whole country. Water tanks like Sea of Parakrama and Minneriya tank , that situated in Polonnaruwa still

flourishing because of ancient man's advanced technology.

Trade and agriculture flourished under the period of Jayabahu 1 in

Polonnaruwa.In today the ancient city of Polonnaruwa remains lot of

ruins such as Watadageya , Thiwanka pilima geya , Gal viharaya and

Palace of King Parakramabahu etc. Today we can Polonnaruwa town with

several hotels , shops for tourists. Hotels such as Royal Lotus hotel

Polonnaruwa , Giritale Hotel , Hotel pahro Polonnaruwa are best luxourius

hotels that can be seen in Polonnaruwa.
