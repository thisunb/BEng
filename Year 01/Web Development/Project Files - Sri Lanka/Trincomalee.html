Trincomalee

Trincomalee is one of famous place for tourism and travel in SriLanka. The reasons for Trincomalee become more popular in tourists because of

natural harbour,bay of Trincomalee , Kanniya hot spring wells etc. Trincomalee is 264KM far away from Colombo. In ancient period Trincomalee

named as 'Gokanna.' Trincomalee natural harbour is more famous because it gives good protection from enemy attacks there for we defeat

enemies very easily. So in this reason lot foreign countries trying to get harbour for their own. It creates tourist attraction also. Beautiful sea

scenarios creates well pleasure in traveller's eyes.

Kanniya bridge and hot spring wells are another place that tourists are

like to visit. It is the longest bridge in Sri Lanka. As well as kanniya hot

spring wells are another historical and natural resource situated in

Trincomalee. These resources are getting tourist's attraction and make

more income for our country.
